Trait Rarity System

Each MegaPython trait has a specific rarity that determines how commonly it appears in the collection. The rarer the trait, the more unique your Python becomes!

  • Common (50%+): Appears frequently
  • Uncommon (20-49%): Less frequently seen
  • Rare (5-19%): Hard to find
  • Legendary (1-4%): Extremely rare
  • Mythic (<1%): Nearly one-of-a-kind
