ABOUT MEGAPYTHON

MegaPython is a collection of 2,222 pixel snakes on the MegaETH blockchain testnet. This project experiments with art, lore, and memes — building the playful Reptiles Universe one hiss at a time.

Each Python is uniquely generated with various traits and attributes. Our collection features 10 background colors, 8 body patterns, 12 eye styles, and 5 rarity tiers.
